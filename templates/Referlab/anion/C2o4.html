Reaction

Take 1 mL of water extract or sodium carbonate extract acidified with acetic acid and add calcium chloride solution. A white precipitate insoluble in ammonium oxalate and oxalic acid solution but soluble in dilute hydrochloric acid and dilute nitric acid is formed. Take the precipitate from the above test and dissolve it in dilute H2SO4 . Add very dilute solution of KMnO4 and warm. Colour of KMnO4 solution is discharged.

Inference Observed

Pass the gas coming out through lime water. The lime water turns milky.
