Reaction

Take 0.1 g of salt in a china dish. Add 1 mL of ethanol and 0.2 mL conc. H2SO4 and heat. Fruity odour confirms the presence of acetate ion. Take 0.1 g of salt in a test tube, add 1-2 mL distilled water, shake well filter if necessary. Add 1 to 2 mL neutral ferric chloride solution to the filtrate.

Inference Observed

Deep red colour appears which disappears on boiling and a brown-red precipitate is formed.
